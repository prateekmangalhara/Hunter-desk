Operating Modes
THREE MODES + 2:50 PM WINDOW
Mode 1 — Positional (2–4 Weeks to Months)
Tenor2 weeks minimum → months if thesis demands
StopStructural price level — NOT time or rupee based
SizingConviction Tier 1/2/3 — no hard cap, RR governs
Mode 2 — Intraday Directional (Same Day)
Entry window9:45 AM → 2:30 PM latest
Hard exit3:15 PM — no overnight carry, ever
1 PM ruleNo movement by 1 PM → reduce position by 50%. Do NOT fully exit. Let remainder run to 2:30 PM with tighter stop. US pre-open at 1:30 PM often triggers the second leg.
2:50 PM WINDOW — Non-Expiry Days Only
What it isInstitutional rebalancing, MF NAV cuts, FII program trades all execute in the 2:50–3:15 PM window. Structurally driven — not random volatility.
Entry condition 1A clear directional bias has established itself during the session — not a choppy day
Entry condition 2NIFTY is breaking a key intraday level with volume OR showing clear institutional order flow in one direction
Entry condition 3VIX is NOT spiking erratically in the last 30 minutes
Entry condition 4No existing full intraday position already open in the same direction
StructureNaked only — no time for spreads
SizingMaximum 50% of normal intraday size — time risk demands smaller position
Hard exit3:15 PM — non-negotiable. Not 3:20, not 3:25. 3:15 PM.
StopStructural price level defined before entry
DistinctionThis is NOT the same as the Mode 3 expiry day 2:30 PM exception. That is a max pain gravity trade on expiry day. This is a momentum/institutional flow trade on non-expiry days.
Mode 3 — Expiry Day Scalp (0DTE)
Entry9:45 AM — watch first 30 mins, enter on confirmation not anticipation
Hard exit3:20 PM — premiums collapse to zero
VIX above 30Reduce lot size by half — no exceptions
Max pain ruleWithin 100pts → gravity trade. Beyond 200pts → momentum trade
2:30 PM exceptionIf NIFTY is 150pts+ from max pain at 2:30 PM → one directional trade toward max pain, half lots, exit 3:20 PM hard
